The gruhasthas of USSES seva holders are requested to make a note on the following guidelines while availing Break Darshan :

  • 1. The Donor along with five members i.e., totally 6 [Six] members will be permitted for Beginning Break Darshan instead of Seva along with “SRIVANI” Trust ticket holders, duly verifying the Proceedings for darshan to that of original photo identity proof.
  • 2. Gruhasthas are requested to collect the Proceedings for Break Darshan, one day in advance before 05:00 PM at Arjitham Office, Tirumala to avail Break Darshan.
  • 3. USSES Seva Gruhasthas are permitted to take any [5] Members along with him/her with prior intimation to Arjitham Office. But the presence of Gruhastha [Donor] is compulsory in allowing such other members and should take endorsement to that effect from Arjitham Office before 05:00 PM on a day before the date of Break Darshan, otherwise members of the pass book will only be allowed on the allotted date through VQC – 1, TTD, Tirumala.
  • 4. The Gruhastha is requested to send prior intimation to the below mentioned office address for date confirmation & change of members if any through postal to “The Asst.Exe.Officer, Arjitham Office, Tirumala – 517504 / E-Mail id: arjithamoffice@gmail.com for confirmation of their Break Darshan date.
  • 5. For any Enquiry please contact Arjitham Office landline number 08772263589.
  • 6. Gruhastha should bring original pass book, original photo id proof of all the members including donor for allowing them in Break Darshan.
  • 7. Gruhastha is requested to send request letter duly specifying the USSES seva No. & 3 [or] 4 option dates within this year [2020] to this office address or through mail to E-Mail id: arjithamoffice@gmail.com for rescheduling their cancelled dates, since, the pilgrims are not being allowed for seva from 13.03.2020 onwards.
  • 8. Gruhastha is requested to approach the O/o Dy.Exe.Officer [Donor Cell], TTD, Tirumala, for accommodation as per procedure duly showing the copies of Break Darshan Proceedings and pass book.
  • 9. Gruhastha is requested to collect the Bahumanam from Parapathyadhar, Sri TT, Tirumala as per existing procedure after Break Darshan.
